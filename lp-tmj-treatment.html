We Start with a Thorough
One-on-One Conversation

Most of our patients have been to many other doctors looking for answers before they visit us. They have completed lots of tests and are not sure if anyone will be able to help them. That is why we start by listening. We gather insights from your experiences and available records. Then, we work with you to develop a practical path forward. Our goal is to provide short-term relief while moving towards a long-term solution to your TMJ / TMD pain and dysfunction. Together, we can get there.

Custom Intra Oral Splints

A Splint is custom-made for each patient and provides relief by allowing the chewing muscles to relax and create a new comfortable bite. In most cases, the process takes 4-6 months.

Orthodontics

Misalignment of your teeth and jaws can be a “hidden” cause of your TMJ pain. As an Orthodontist, Dr. Hall is uniquely qualified to diagnose and treat TMJ, then combine orthodontic tooth movement into a TMJ solutions.

Occlusal Equilibration

The process of conservatively reshaping the teeth to balance the bite forces and allow the teeth to come into contact at the same time for optimal function.

Meet Dr. Tom Hall

Dr. Hall attended the University of Texas Dental Branch in Houston and earned a Doctor of Dental Surgery degree. In addition, he obtained a Specialty Certificate in Orthodontics from the University of Texas Dental Branch. During his orthodontic residency, he completed additional course work and original research in TMD, bite alignment, and muscle function to earn a Master of Science degree from the University of Texas School of Public Health. Dr. Hall’s original research on TMJ/TMD was published in the American Journal of Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics.
